The GSP Audit 2016, like every year, is a combination of knowledge, fun activities and learning, where you would not only be able to understand your environment better, but also take steps to conserve precious natural resources and be a change maker!

Here are some tips to remember before you start the audit:

  • Identify a team of around 10 students for each of the six sections of the Audit
  • Each section will be supervised by teachers and administrative staffs
  • Try to answer the subjective type of questions first (Yes/ No)
  • Then identify the questions that require monitoring over a period of time
  • Remember to send pictures of teams conducting the audit
  • Remember, the FAQs are always there for your help at:
    www.greenschoolsprogramme.org/faqs/
